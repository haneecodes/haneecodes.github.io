Plyometric Workout: No Equipment Needed

1.) Start With Stretches.

15 Forward Lunges (Each Leg).

15 Leg Swings (Each Leg). This will help loosen the hips and hamstrings

Plyometrics (Atleast 3 Days A Week):

2 Sets of 15: Kneeling Jump Squats

3 Sets of 10: Pogo Hops

3 Sets of 8: Split Jumps

3 Sets 8 Squat Jumps
